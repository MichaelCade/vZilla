About vZilla

I am a Systems Engineer at Veeam Software in the UK, covering mainly London, The Midlands and East Anglia.

    •Provide on-site and web-based demonstrations of Veeam software.
    •Teach partner SE’s & Architects about Veeam solutions.
    •Team with Veeam’s partners to provide training, product demonstrations and lead customer meetings.
    •Build relationships with customers and partners.
    •Assist sales to help secure and qualify new Veeam partners.
    •Working closely with the Veeam development team by providing customer feedback and your ideas.
    •Empower and assist customers while they evaluate Veeam’s product offerings.

Given my background I also have a strong focus around increasing our value add around our Alliance partnerships, namely this includes Cisco & NetApp for the UK regions.

I am very active on social media, twitter being my main outlet. I also write a blog as often as possible to share my views, new releases and just general how to content. From being active on Twitter and other social media I have been a member of the NetApp Advocate program, (The NetApp A Team) for well over one year now and I have recently been enrolled on the Cisco Champions class of 2015. This will mean a stronger focus on writing about NetApp and Cisco over the other technology areas but it doesn't limit me to that.



Throughout my career I have been very prudent in collecting and learning as many new skills as possible, I really enjoy training but also conversing with my peers about new products, issues and generally talking about the industry happenings. I also take a lot of pride in obtaining the latest accreditations and certifications for each vendor I work closely with, as a lead consultant I need to always make sure I am one page at least ahead of my peers and customers, and to do this training and certifications allows me to do this.

Currently I am working through completing my Veeam VMCE, I then have a long list other training and certifications I am looking to complete.

Outside of "Work" I enjoy time with my family, my wife Gemma and my ever growing son Jack, I also play rugby union currently signed for a national one side in England playing within the top 90 English clubs.
